# Phase 5: Power BI Dashboard | HR Attrition Analysis

---

## 1. Overview

A Power BI dashboard was developed to analyze HR attrition trends and present interactive insights on employee demographics, departments, job roles, salary, experience, and workforce characteristics for data-driven HR decision-making.

---

## 2. Objectives

- Monitor employee attrition.
- Track key HR KPIs.
- Analyze workforce trends.
- Identify high-risk employee groups.
- Support retention planning through interactive dashboards.

---

## 3. Dashboard Audience

- Human Resources (HR)
- HR Managers
- Talent Acquisition Team
- Department Managers
- Senior Management
- Business Leadership

---

## 14. Business Recommendations

- Prioritize retention for high-attrition departments.
- Monitor employees with frequent overtime.
- Improve work-life balance initiatives.
- Review compensation strategies.
- Support employee development through training and promotions.
- Monitor HR KPIs continuously.

---

## 16. Limitations

- Based on historical employee data.
- External workforce factors are excluded.
- Attrition reasons are unavailable.
- Insights depend on dataset quality.

## 17. Future Enhancements

## 18. Conclusion

The Power BI dashboard provides an interactive view of employee attrition through KPIs and visualizations, enabling HR teams to monitor workforce trends, identify high-risk employee groups, and support informed retention decisions.
